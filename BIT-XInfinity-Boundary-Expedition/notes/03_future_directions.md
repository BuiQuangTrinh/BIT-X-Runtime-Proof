# Overview

BIT-X∞ is designed as an open expeditionary research axis.

Its future is intentionally left open-ended.

The repository does not assume that all discoveries will:
- belong to the BIT core,
- remain useful forever,
- or evolve along a single fixed direction.

Instead, BIT-X∞ is expected to continuously:
- expand,
- restructure,
- absorb,
- discard,
- and rediscover
new runtime abstractions and adaptive coordination ideas over time.
